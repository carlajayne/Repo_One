Pollution Probe

Pollution Probe is a Canadian non-profit organization that is a leading agent of change at the intersection of communities, health and the environment. The goal for this project is to redesign the website with a focus on the information architecture improvements and the website’s overall visual and usability.

E-Plan

A mobile design project which focuses on creating a solution on user’s day to day commitments and activities during the pandemic. It specializes in upscaling a planning mobile application to integration of services from professionals.

Webtrepraneur

A project that helps small business owners expand and continuously evolve with this new way of earning profit. Webtrapreneur is web design agency that elevates businesses’ online presence to gain more buyers and increase profitability as they adjust to the limitations the Global Pandemic brought to them.
